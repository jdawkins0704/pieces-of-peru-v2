About Pieces of Peru

A hospitality brand grounded in culture.

Pieces of Peru is built around the idea that Peruvian cuisine can be expressed in different ways while still feeling connected through warmth, craft, and shared identity. Each restaurant offers its own atmosphere and occasion, while contributing to a larger brand experience.

Brand point of view

Distinct in mood. Unified in spirit.

Whether the moment calls for a more refined night out or something lively and casual, the brand is designed to meet guests with a sense of intention, character, and memorable hospitality.
